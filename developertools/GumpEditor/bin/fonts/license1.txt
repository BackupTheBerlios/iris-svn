                       BUNCHLÓ 3.0

Cló  Gaelach i leagan amach TrueType le haghaidh  Windows
is  ea  Bunchló.  Cloíonn sé leis an gcaighdeán a leagadh
síos don tábla códála Rómhánach Ceilteach Leathnaithe  do
Windows  (féach an t-alt le Michael Everson  dar  teideal
‘Táblaí  códála Ceilteacha: caighdeáin nua’  a  foilsíodh
san  iris ‘Feasta’, Meitheamh 1994)  ach amháin go bhfuil 
siombal an euro curtha isteach in ionad an '#'.

Tá sé ceadaithe  Bunchló a chóipeáil agus a dháileadh gan 
srian  ar bith  ach an téacs-chomhad seo  a bheith leis i 
gcónaí. Is ceadaithe freisin é a úsáid saor in aisce chun 
ábhar  ar bith  sna teangacha Gaelacha,  nó ábhar  neamh-
-thráchtála  in  aon  teanga  eile  a  fhoilsiú.   NÍL sé 
ceadaithe Bunchló a úsáid chun  ábhar tráchtála i dteanga 
neamh-Ghaelach a fhoilsiú  gan cead a fháil uaimse  roimh
ré.

Vincent Morley
Baile Átha Cliath

                           ***

Bunchló  is a Gaelic font in TrueType format for Windows.
It conforms to the standard for the extended Roman Celtic
code  table  for  Windows  (see the  article  by  Michael
Everson  entitled  ‘Táblaí códála Ceilteacha:  caighdeáin
nua’  in ‘Feasta’, June 1994) except that the euro symbol
has been substituted for the '#' sign.

Bunchló  may  be  copied   and  distributed  without  any 
restriction provided  it is accompanied  at  all times by 
this text file.  It may also  be used free of  charge for 
the publication of  any material in the  Gaelic languages 
and of non-commercial material in any other language.  It 
may  NOT  be  used  for  the  publication  of  commercial 
material  in  any  non-Gaelic language  without  my prior 
permission.
